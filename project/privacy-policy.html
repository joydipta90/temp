  • Privacy Policy

Welcome to Feepran! We're committed to baking delectable cakes and to making sure your private information remains just that: private. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information provided to and collected from you when you interact with our online cake shop.

What do we collect?

We may collect the following when you visit Feepran, and we use them to make your cake-picking journey more enjoyable:

  • Browsing information: The pages that you visit, items that you view, and search history make us understand your favorite cakes.
  • Order details: Any time you are placing an order, we'll collect your name, shipping address, and your email address in order to guarantee safe delivery of your delicious products.
  • Communication details: If ever you contact us with questions or feedback, then we might be keeping a record of the conversation so that the next time around, we'd be able to assist you well.

We use the information you provide in a few ways

  • To fulfill your cake cravings: We require your information to process your orders and deliver your preferred cakes fresh to your doorstep.
  • Personalize your experience: We understand your browsing habits, and thus we can recommend cakes you might enjoy, making your search for the perfect cake a breeze.
  • Improve our service: Your comments and browsing patterns are great tools for us to improve our website and cake selection.

We will not sell or give your information to hungry strangers!

Feepran respects your privacy. We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third-party advertisers or other organizations without your permission.

Protecting your information

We have taken the responsibility of safeguarding your information very seriously. We will use industry standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

How do you control your information?

You have choices as per the PDPO of Hong Kong. You can:

  • Access your information: Feel free to contact us at info@feepran.com to request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Update your information: If your details change, let us know, and we'll happily update our records.
  • Unsubscribe from emails: To not receive more messages from Feepran, simply click the link in the messages we send to you to opt out of the service.

Amendments to Privacy Policy

Feepran may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. To check for updates please visit our site periodically.
